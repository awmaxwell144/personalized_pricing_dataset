American Airlines Retailing

Inspiring the future of airline retailing through modern and tailored experiences


American Airlines leads the travel industry with a customer-centric focus through modern retailing offers and seamless experiences.

In the past, customers who booked travel through third-party distribution partners may have had subpar booking and travel management experiences with limited access to fares and packages.

Today, American’s journey to modern retailing gives customers access to Enhanced content and functionality enabled by New Distribution Capability (NDC). These enhancements simplify the customer experience by enabling us to distribute products in new ways and provide more relevant, tailored offers to our customers. The International Air Transportation Association (IATA) designated American Airlines with Airline Retailing Maturity (ARM) status based on our retailing capabilities, partnerships development and value capture.

Travel agency connection & implementation options

American has worked closely with major technology partners to provide you access to our modern retailing ecosystem. We're committed to delivering modern retailing experiences to elevate your travel journey.

This is how you can get connected:

Via GDS/NDC Aggregator

NDC Aggregators are third-party technology companies such as Global Distribution Systems (GDSs), Travel Management Companies (TMCs) or Online Booking Tools (OBTs) specializing in NDC integrations with American and other airlines. Tickets issued via this connection option are issued on neutral ticket stock.

Recommended for travel agencies, preferring an off-the-shelf solution that doesn't require IT resource deployment for integration.

View our approved technology partners.

Via Travel Agency Direct Connect

Travel Agency Direct Connect allows travel agencies to connect directly to our API. This connection type requires an ARC/IATA accredited agency for ticketing and these tickets are issued on neutral ticket stock.

Recommended for large travel agencies, TMCs and technology providers with IT resources available to complete integration and preferring to manage the technology. Integration projects are expected to take between 3-6 months to complete, but may vary depending on business needs.

Our dedicated team offers project roadmap and production support as well as assistance for other integration queries and needs.

Please fill out our contact form to get started.

 

Corporate connection & implementation options

American has worked closely with major technology partners to provide you access to our modern retailing ecosystem. We are committed to delivering modern retailing experiences to elevate your travel journey.

This is how you can get connected:

Via GDS/NDC Aggregator

NDC Aggregators are third-party technology companies such as Global Distribution Systems (GDS), Travel Management Companies (TMC) or Online Booking Tools (OBT) specializing in NDC integrations with American and other airlines. Tickets issued via this connection option are issued on neutral ticket stock.

Recommended for corporations utilizing a travel agency and preferring an off-the-shelf solution that doesn’t require IT resource deployment for integration.

View American’s approved technology partners »

Via Direct Booking

Direct Booking is the fastest solution, enabling access to NDC content for corporations or small-medium-sized businesses.

Implementation is not required; however, NDC-enabled booking tool adoption may be necessary.

Direct Booking tickets are issued on American Airlines ticket stock and American will become the Agency of Record for all ticketing and servicing inquiries.
